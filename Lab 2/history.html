Founding

Real Madrid was founded on March 6, 1902, by the brothers Padros.

First La Liga Title

Real Madrid won their first La Liga title in the 1931-32 season.

European Dominance

In the 1950s, Real Madrid dominated European football by winning the first five European Cups.

Recent Success

In recent years, Real Madrid has continued its success, including winning multiple Champions League titles.
